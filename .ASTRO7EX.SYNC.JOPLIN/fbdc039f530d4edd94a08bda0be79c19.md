## Operational Outcome

Because the invariants are enforced:

- Hope feels precious rather than triumphant
- Political critique remains grounded and unsentimental
- Violence never becomes spectacle
- The ending preserves uncertainty without nihilism

## Summary

*Children of Men* maintains coherence by enforcing **Non-Negotiable Invariants** that ensure:

- hope is fragile
- systems brutalize under pressure
- survival is morally neutral
- humanity persists only in small acts

The film works because **the future is possible, not promised**.

**If hope were guaranteed, Children of Men would be a fairy tale.**
