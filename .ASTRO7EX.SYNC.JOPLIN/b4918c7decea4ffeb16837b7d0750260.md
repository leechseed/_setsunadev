- **Purpose**: Glute and pelvic floor activation

- **Purpose**: Passive openness
- **Cues**:

  - Knees drop wide
  - Hands behind head or on belly
  - Let gravity do the work

### **8. Bridge Pose (Setu Bandhasana)**
