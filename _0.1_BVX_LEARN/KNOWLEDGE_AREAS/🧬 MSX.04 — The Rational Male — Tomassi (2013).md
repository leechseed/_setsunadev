## CORE THESIS
Understanding women's pluralistic sexual strategy (hypergamy) and the social conventions that obscure it is prerequisite to men navigating intersexual dynamics with informed agency rather than idealized delusion.

---

## INVARIANTS
*These are the unchanging principles — true regardless of context.*

1. **Hypergamy is the operating system.** Women's mating strategy optimizes for the highest-value male available — this drive doesn't switch off inside relationships, marriages, or commitments.
2. **Frame determines the relationship.** Every interaction operates inside someone's reality — you are either living in your frame or hers, and the one who holds frame sets the terms.
3. **A man's value peaks later than a woman's.** Male SMV curves upward through the 30s (status, resources, competence accumulate); female SMV peaks earlier. Understanding the timeline changes strategic decisions about commitment.
4. **The person with fewer options holds less power.** Abundance — real or perceived — is not a mindset trick; it's a structural position that determines negotiating leverage in every relationship.
5. **Men are the romantics, women are the pragmatists.** Men idealize love and loyalty; women's love is conditioned on a man's continued demonstration of value. These are different operating systems, not moral judgments.

## CORE CONCEPTS

**1. Hypergamy**
Women's biological and psychological drive to secure the highest-value male available. Operates on two tracks: short-term (genetic/physical attraction — "Alpha Fucks") and long-term (provisioning/security — "Beta Bucks"). These dual imperatives create the tension at the center of most intersexual conflicts.

**2. Sexual Market Value (SMV)**
A composite measure of an individual's desirability. Male SMV is weighted toward status, competence, resources, and physicality — and peaks in the mid-30s. Female SMV is weighted toward youth, fertility signals, and physical attractiveness — and peaks earlier. Understanding the curves changes timing decisions about commitment and self-investment.

**3. Frame**
The psychological reality within which an interaction takes place. Whoever's frame is accepted as "normal" controls the dynamic. Frame is not dominance — it's the ambient narrative. Losing frame means operating under someone else's assumptions, values, and emotional terms.

**4. Plate Theory**
Maintaining multiple romantic prospects simultaneously — not as deception, but as a structural hedge against ONEitis and scarcity. The purpose is abundance mentality through lived reality: when you have options, neediness drops, and your behavior with each individual woman improves naturally.

**5. The Iron Rules**
Nine non-negotiable principles including: frame is everything; never reveal your partner count; a woman who makes you wait for sex never valued the sex enough; never self-deprecate; it's always better to develop new prospects than to reconstruct a failed relationship. Intended as bright-line rules for men prone to rationalizing self-defeating behavior.

**6. The Feminine Imperative**
Tomassi's term for the collection of social conventions that prioritize women's sexual strategy as the default moral framework. Examples: shaming male sexuality, framing male self-interest as misogyny, positioning female approval as the measure of male worth. Understanding it is the first step to operating outside it.
