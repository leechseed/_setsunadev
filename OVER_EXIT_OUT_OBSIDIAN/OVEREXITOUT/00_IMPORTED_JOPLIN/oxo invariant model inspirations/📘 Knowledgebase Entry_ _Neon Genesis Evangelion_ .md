## Failure Tests (What Dies)

The following are structurally disallowed:

- Heroic mastery resolving trauma  
- Stable, unified identity as an endpoint  
- Intimacy without risk or pain  
- Salvation through power or transcendence  
- Clean psychological catharsis

## Operational Outcome

Because the invariants are enforced:

- Mecha spectacle never overrides interior conflict
- Mythic elements serve psychology, not lore
- Endings vary in form but not in truth
- Scale expansion deepens, rather than dilutes, meaning

## Summary

*Neon Genesis Evangelion* maintains coherence by enforcing **Non-Negotiable Invariants** that ensure:

- connection always hurts
- identity never stabilizes
- avoidance always backfires
- power never heals

The series works because it refuses false resolution.

**If healing were easy, Evangelion would not exist.**
