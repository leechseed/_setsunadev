## Overview

This document defines the **Non-Negotiable Invariants** that enforce coherence across scale in *End of Watch*.

This is not a buddy-cop comedy.  
It is not a law-and-order morality play.  
It is a structural account of **how proximity to violence reshapes intimacy and how routine heroism accumulates lethal consequence**.

These invariants remain true across:
- dashcam and bodycam footage  
- casual patrol banter  
- domestic moments  
- gang escalation  
- the final ambush  

They are **constraints on meaning**, not stylistic realism.

## Failure Tests (What Dies)

The following are structurally disallowed:

- Violence appearing only during action scenes  
- Brotherhood without shared exposure  
- Competence framed as protection  
- Institutions preventing personal tragedy  
- Endings that reframe loss as purpose  

If any appear, *End of Watch* collapses into cop myth.

---

## Operational Outcome

Because these invariants are enforced:

- The found-footage style carries emotional weight  
- Humor and warmth intensify dread rather than dilute it  
- The partnership feels authentic and fragile  
- Violence feels sudden, not earned  
- The ending lands as inevitability, not shock
