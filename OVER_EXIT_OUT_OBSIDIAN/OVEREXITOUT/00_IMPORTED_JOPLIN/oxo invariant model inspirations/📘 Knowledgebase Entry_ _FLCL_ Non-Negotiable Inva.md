## Failure Tests (What Dies)

The following elements are structurally disallowed:

- Clear explanations of events  
- Adults acting as moral authorities  
- Power without emotional cost  
- Growth portrayed as clean or linear  
- Normalcy framed as safety or comfort  

If any appear, *FLCL* collapses into noise instead of meaning.

## Operational Outcome

Because these invariants are enforced:

- Chaos reads as intentional  
- Comedy carries emotional weight  
- Symbolism remains legible without exposition  
- Rewatching deepens understanding  
- Adolescence feels honest rather than idealized
