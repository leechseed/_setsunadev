## Overview

This document defines the **Non-Negotiable Invariants** that enforce coherence across scale in *Pain & Gain*.

This is not a heist comedy.  
It is not a satire that softens consequence.  
It is a structural account of **how aspiration mutates into brutality when intelligence is replaced by entitlement and belief substitutes for competence**.

These invariants remain true across:
- motivational monologues  
- amateur criminal planning  
- escalating violence  
- legal reckoning  
- the final moral accounting  

They are **constraints on meaning**, not tonal jokes.

## Failure Tests (What Dies)

The following are structurally disallowed:

- Criminals succeeding through confidence alone  
- Irony excusing brutality  
- Violence framed as competence  
- Aspiration treated as moral claim  
- Endings that soften accountability  

If any appear, *Pain & Gain* collapses into empty farce.

---

## Operational Outcome

Because these invariants are enforced:

- Comedy sharpens horror instead of blunting it  
- Characters read as delusional, not clever  
- Violence feels grotesque rather than empowering  
- The true story framing lands as indictment  
- The ending restores reality without mercy

## Summary

*Pain & Gain* maintains coherence by enforcing brutal constraints:

- belief without skill is lethal  
- aspiration does not entitle  
- violence masks incompetence  
- reality always returns  

The film works because **it refuses to let confidence substitute for consequence**.

**If wanting made you worthy, *Pain & Gain* would be inspirational.**
