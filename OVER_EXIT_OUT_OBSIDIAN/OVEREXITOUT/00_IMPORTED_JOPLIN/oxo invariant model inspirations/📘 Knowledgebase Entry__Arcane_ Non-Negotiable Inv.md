## Failure Tests (What Dies)

The following are structurally disallowed:

- Technological salvation without collateral damage  
- Love as a protective force against consequence  
- Institutional reform solving inequality  
- Violence producing lasting peace  
- Individual heroism overriding systemic pressure  

Any such element would violate invariants and collapse coherence.

---

## Operational Outcome

Because the invariants are enforced:

- Emotional arcs remain grounded in consequence
- Technological spectacle reinforces political tragedy
- Character victories feel costly, not triumphant
- Expansion across seasons preserves thematic integrity

## Summary

*Arcane* maintains coherence by enforcing **Non-Negotiable Invariants** that ensure:

- progress always harms unevenly
- love increases stakes
- systems harden inequality
- violence compounds conflict

The series works because **nothing improves without someone paying the price**.

**If progress healed everyone, Arcane would not exist.**
