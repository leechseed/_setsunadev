## Overview

This document defines the **Non-Negotiable Invariants** that enforce coherence across scale in *Slumdog Millionaire*.

This is not a rags-to-riches fairy tale.  
It is not a meritocracy fantasy.  
It is a structural account of **how survival imprints knowledge and how love persists through systemic cruelty**.

These invariants remain true across:
- interrogation scenes  
- game-show spectacle  
- episodic flashbacks  
- urban violence and chance  
- the final public reckoning  

They are **constraints on meaning**, not sentimental framing.

## Failure Tests (What Dies)

The following are structurally disallowed:

- Genius revealed as hidden talent  
- Suffering reframed as moral training  
- Luck portrayed as destiny  
- Love rewarded with protection  
- Victory equated with justice  

If any appear, *Slumdog Millionaire* collapses into sentimental myth.

## Summary

*Slumdog Millionaire* maintains coherence by enforcing hard constraints:

- experience creates knowledge  
- systems contest chance  
- love persists without safety  
- escape does not equal justice  

The film works because **memory, not merit, carries the story forward**.

**If winning fixed the world, *Slumdog Millionaire* would be a lie.**
