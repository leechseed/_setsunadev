## Failure Tests (What Dies)

The following are structurally disallowed:

- Power as moral proof  
- Destiny excusing atrocity  
- Final victory ending political cycles  
- Violence as lasting solution  
- Empire defeated without replacement risk  

Any such element would violate invariants and fracture the myth.

## Summary

*Star Wars* maintains coherence by enforcing **Non-Negotiable Invariants** that ensure:
