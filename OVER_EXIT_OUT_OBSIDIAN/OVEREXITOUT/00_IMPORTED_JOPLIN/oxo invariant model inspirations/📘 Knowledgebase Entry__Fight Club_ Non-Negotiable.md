- Violence as moral purification  
- Charismatic leadership as liberation  
- Rebellion producing authentic freedom  
- Identity resolved through negation  
- Nihilism delivering clarity or peace

## Operational Outcome

Because the invariants are enforced:

- The film critiques what it depicts
- Escalation reads as pathology, not triumph
- Ideology collapses under its own logic
- Spectacle exposes emptiness rather than filling it

The work remains coherent because **every solution fails by design**.

## Summary

*Fight Club* maintains coherence by enforcing **Non-Negotiable Invariants** that ensure:

- identity fractures under negation
- violence excites but never heals
- charisma conceals domination
- rebellion reproduces systems

The story works because it refuses to validate its own seductions.

**If violence gave meaning, Fight Club would be propaganda.**
