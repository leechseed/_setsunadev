# 1. **Overview**

An astrology chart is a **structured personality engine**.
For narrative, it acts as a **character blueprint generator** that creates:

* Personality
* Emotional wiring
* Love style
* Ambition
* Trauma
* Fate arc
* Role in story

Every element of the chart has a specific **storytelling function.**

This knowledgebase explains **each item**, **definition**, and **narrative purpose**.

---

# 2. **Core Identity Components**

## 2.1 **Sun**

**Definition:** The ego, will, identity, core drive.
**Narrative Function:** What the character *believes they are*; their self-concept and thematic motivation.

* Drives the *main goal*
* Defines the archetypal ‚Äúclass‚Äù of the character
* Determines what they fight for

---

## 2.2 **Moon**

**Definition:** Emotional system, instinct, subconscious needs.
**Narrative Function:** The character‚Äôs **damage**, **emotional triggers**, and **reaction style**.

* How they respond under pressure
* Attachment style
* Core wound and emotional survival pattern

The Moon is the psychological engine behind all conflict.

---

## 2.3 **Rising / Ascendant**

**Definition:** The mask, persona, first impression, mode of action.
**Narrative Function:** How the character **enters scenes**, communicates, and carries themselves.

* Determines aesthetic
* Determines social posture
* Determines narrative *presence*

The ASC tells you what the audience notices instantly.

---

## 2.4 **Chart Shape**

**Definition:** The distribution of planets in the wheel (Bowl, Splash, Bundle, etc.)
**Narrative Function:** The character‚Äôs **scope**, **focus**, and psychic limits.

Examples:

* **Bowl:** Intense, limited focus, self-contained
* **Splash:** Chaotic, diverse interests
* **Locomotive:** Driven, mission-oriented

# 3. **Planetary Placements**

Each planet = **a subsystem of the psyche**.
Sign = *style of expression*.
House = *area of life*.
Degree = *flavor/subtype*.

---

## 3.1 **Personal Planets**

These build the **moment-to-moment personality**.

### **Sun**

Identity, will, conscious ego.

### **Moon**

Emotion, instinct, subconscious drives.

### **Mercury**

Thinking style, communication, cognition.

**Narrative:**

* How they speak
* How they solve problems
* Intellectual strengths/weaknesses

### **Venus**

Love, pleasure, aesthetic taste, values.

**Narrative:**

* Romance style
* What they find beautiful
* Their values and ethics in relationships

### **Mars**

Action, aggression, sexuality, ambition.

**Narrative:**

* How they fight
* How they assert
* Sexual drive style
* Strategic aggression

---

## 3.2 **Social Planets**

These shape **worldview and strategy.**

### **Jupiter**

Growth, idealism, luck, opportunities.

Narrative:

* Moral compass
* Long-term optimism/pessimism
* Their gifts and blessings

### **Saturn**

Limits, discipline, fear, karma.

Narrative:

* Core fear
* Burdens
* Long-term struggle
* Life lesson

---

## 3.3 **Outer Planets**

These are **generational forces** that create deeper arcs.

### **Uranus**

Rebellion, awakening, chaos.

### **Neptune**

Dreams, illusions, addictions, spirituality.

### **Pluto**

Power, trauma, transformation, obsession.

Narrative:

* Shadow arc
* Collective forces
* Trauma patterns
* Apocalypse buttons in their psyche

---

## 3.4 **Points**

These are non-planet markers with huge narrative weight.

### **Ascendant (ASC)**

Mask, body, aura.

### **Midheaven (MC)**

Public image, career destiny, mythic role.

### **North Node**

Life path evolution.

### **South Node**

Past-life habits, karmic baggage.

### **Chiron**

Wound that never fully heals; healer archetype.

Narrative:

* Their lifelong wound
* Their role as healer or wounded one

# 5. **Aspects**

Aspects are **relationships between planets**, forming the **wiring diagram** of the psyche.

| Aspect      | Meaning               | Narrative Function         |
| ----------- | --------------------- | -------------------------- |
| Conjunction | Fused energies        | Obsessions or superpowers  |
| Sextile     | Opportunities         | Easy skill growth          |
| Square      | Conflict, friction    | Inner tension; plot engine |
| Trine       | Talent, flow          | Natural genius areas       |
| Opposition  | Polarity, projections | Shadow relationships       |

Aspects create the **inner conflicts** that drive character arcs.
