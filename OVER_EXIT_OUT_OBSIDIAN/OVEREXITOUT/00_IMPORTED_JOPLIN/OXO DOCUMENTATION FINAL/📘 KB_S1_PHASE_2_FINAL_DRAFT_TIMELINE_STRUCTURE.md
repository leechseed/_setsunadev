## 1. Track Configuration (The Lanes)

In Final Draft's Timeline, create **three distinct lanes**. This allows you to see the "Scramble" in real-time.

- **Lane 1: Chronological Order (The Truth):** Every beat of the story from Victoria's birth to the Reset in Movement 6, placed in the exact order they occurred.
    
- **Lane 2: Broadcast Order (The Puzzle):** This is where you drag and drop beats to form the 10 episodes. This is what the script pages will actually reflect.
    
- **Lane 3: The "Asset" State (System Logic):** A lane dedicated to Victoria's hardware status (Acquisition, Integration, Awareness). This ensures the "Glitch Integrity" policy is met.

## 3. The "Ghost" Beat Strategy

In Final Draft, use "Ghost Beats" (Beats that aren't yet assigned to a scene) to mark **Information Gates**.

- **The "Cyborg" reveal** should be a Red Ghost Beat in Lane 1 at the "Bourne" mark.
    
- You then look at Lane 2 and ensure no beats _before_ Episode 6 in the broadcast order contain information that would "break" that Ghost Beat.

## 4. Metadata and Tags

For every beat you create in the timeline, use the **Tags** feature to track the following:

- **Invariant:** (e.g., #Shame, #PowerOnBody, #RevolutionIsUndignified)
    
- **PSTO Logic:** (e.g., #RecurringTotem, #TransMovementColdOpen)
    
- **Character Owner:** (e.g., #Bishop, #Montgomery)
