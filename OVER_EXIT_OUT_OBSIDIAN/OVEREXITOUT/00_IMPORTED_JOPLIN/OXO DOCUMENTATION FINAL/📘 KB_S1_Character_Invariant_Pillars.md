## 1. The Power Dynamics Model

In this series, the primary conflict is not a "battle of wills" but a **Systemic Triple-Point**.

- **The Engine (Anna)**: Pushes for growth and escalation at any cost.
    
- **The Filter (Quinn)**: Removes the human "noise" created by the engine.
    
- **The Residue (Tori)**: The person who remains after the filter has passed through.
