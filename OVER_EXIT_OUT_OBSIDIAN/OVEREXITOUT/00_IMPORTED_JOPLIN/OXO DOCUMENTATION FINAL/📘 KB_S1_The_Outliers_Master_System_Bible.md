## 1. The Geography: The Delta Coast Spiral

The world is a concentric "Spiral" spanning Florida, Alabama, and Georgia.

- **The Core**: The Spaceport. The apex of "Legibility." Where the **Bishops** rule.
    
- **The Middle Bands**: The Academy and Industrial Sectors.
    
- **The Outer Bands**: The "Wild West." Lawless everglades and super-highways. Where the **Fowlers** and **Montgomerys** operate.
    

---

## 2. The Reconstruction Logic: Death as a Product Recall

In _The Outliers_, death is not an end; it is a **Boot Sequence**.

- **The Initialization**: When a high-value "Outlier" (Victoria, Quinn, Riley) dies in a catastrophic event, they are acquired by a Faction.
    
- **The Reset**: A "hard-reboot" of the asset where the system overwrites the traumatic memory with a "System-Approved" reality (a funeral, a ceremony).
    
- **The Reveal**: Characters believe they are human until **Movement 4 (Bourne)**, where the hardware becomes undeniable.

## 6. The Invariants of Power (The KLK Framework)

The series adheres to the radical structural constraints of _Kill la Kill_:

1. **Power is Worn on the Body**: Rank is displayed on uniforms; less clothing = more biometric data-transparency.
    
2. **Shame is a Tool of Control**: The Administration uses public social "Desyncs" and sexual blackmail to enforce obedience.
    
3. **Resistance Requires Self-Acceptance**: Victoria wins by embracing her "messy" hardware rather than trying to be "clean."
    
4. **Revolution is Undignified**: The break from the Academy in Movement 4 is chaotic, obscene, and violent.
