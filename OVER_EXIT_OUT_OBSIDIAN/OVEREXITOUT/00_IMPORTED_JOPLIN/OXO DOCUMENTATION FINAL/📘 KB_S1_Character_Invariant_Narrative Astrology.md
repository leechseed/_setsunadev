## Overview: The Power Dynamics Model

The series does **not** depict a conflict of personalities.  
It depicts a **Systemic Triple-Point** where Power, Process, and Human Cost intersect.

### Core Functions

- **The Engine (Anna)**  
  Drives growth, escalation, and consolidation.

- **The Filter (Quinn)**  
  Converts human behavior into legible, enforceable signals.

- **The Residue (Tori)**  
  The human remainder after the system finishes processing.

Supporting stabilizers and fault lines:
- **The Symbol (Hunter)** ‚Äî brand gravity and collapse vector  
- **The Boundary (Penny)** ‚Äî authorization and lawful cover  

Each function maps cleanly to a **Narrative Astrology Sign**.

## Tori Midnight ‚Äî **Scorpio**  
**Invariant:** Cost / Meaning  
**Role:** Protagonist ‚Äî The Residue

### Structural Significance
Scorpio governs:
- Irreversible loss  
- Survival after annihilation  
- Meaning extracted from damage  
- What persists after destruction  

Tori is not adaptive or reformist.  
She is what **remains** once the system has finished enforcing.

### Narrative Function
- Absorbs cost without converting it into leverage  
- Survives through exclusion, not protection  
- Maintains private meaning that cannot affect outcomes  

### Failure Mode
- Every bond increases exposure  
- Survival always requires further sacrifice  
- No loss resolves cleanly  

**Scorpio Law:** Nothing is lost cleanly. Nothing is gained without residue.

## Anna Colson ‚Äî **Capricorn**  
**Invariant:** Power / Escalation  
**Role:** Primary Antagonist ‚Äî The Engine

### Structural Significance
Capricorn governs:
- Institutional continuity  
- Long-term authority  
- Justified harm  
- Growth as obligation  

Anna does not seek chaos or cruelty.  
She seeks **systems that survive expansion**.

### Narrative Function
- Every solution increases complexity  
- Harm is reframed as necessity  
- Stopping escalation would imply error  

### Failure Mode
- Accumulated complexity becomes unsustainable  
- Reduction equals self-negation  

**Capricorn Law:** Power that stops growing is already dead.

## Quinn Bishop ‚Äî **Virgo**  
**Invariant:** Legibility  
**Role:** The Instrument ‚Äî The Filter

### Structural Significance

- Noise reduction
